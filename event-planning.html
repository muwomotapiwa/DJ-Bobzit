Welcome to Your Event Planning Journey

Planning an event can be overwhelming, but with the right tools and guidance, it can also be exciting and rewarding. This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to ensure your special day runs smoothly—from creating timelines to preparing song lists.

Event Planning Checklist

  • 6 Months Before: Book your DJ, venue, and other vendors.
  • 3 Months Before: Finalize guest list and create a rough playlist.
  • 1 Month Before: Confirm all vendor details and finalize the event timeline.
  • 1 Week Before: Share your playlist and any special requests with your DJ.
  • Day Of: Relax and enjoy your event!

Tips for Working with Your DJ

  • Share your playlist and preferences at least 2 weeks before the event.
  • Communicate any special requests (e.g., first dance, announcements).
  • Confirm event details (date, time, location) 1 week prior.
